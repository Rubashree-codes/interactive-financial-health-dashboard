Debt Payoff Strategies
Debt Snowball Method

Pay minimums on all debts, then put extra money toward the smallest balance first. This builds momentum and motivation.

  • Quick psychological wins
  • Builds confidence
  • Reduces number of payments
Debt Avalanche Method

Pay minimums on all debts, then put extra money toward the highest interest rate first. This saves the most money.

  • Saves most money on interest
  • Mathematically optimal
  • Faster total payoff time
Additional Tips
  • Consider debt consolidation if you qualify for lower interest rates
  • Avoid taking on new debt while paying off existing debt
  • Make payments more than the minimum when possible
  • Consider balance transfers to lower interest cards
  • Use windfalls (tax refunds, bonuses) for extra payments
